About Me

Why Fresh Perspective?

Maybe you noticed my blog name or even the "fp18" in my URL ... To me, fresh perspective is the reward for thinking independently.

Take my cover picture. It's awesome, isn't it? Some far-off place full of intrigue and stories ...

Actually, it's in Kansas City! It's a well-known landmark — the WWI Memorial right across from Union Station. But thanks to fresh perspective, it could easily be the portal to another dimension.

As a redheaded left-hander, I was destined to do things differently. I started my career in print publishing, but once I learned HTML, the immediate gratification of making a correction and seeing it online in seconds resonated with me.

When I'm not writing content or researching and writing code, I'm:

  • an adoring mom
  • a cat and all-around animal lover
  • a diehard alternative music fan
  • and a semi-successful gardener

I travel whenever I can and plan to visit my beautiful daughter in April. We're taking the train from Seattle to Portland to catch The 1975 and deeply breathe in that rarified PacWest air. Well I'll be anyway ...

January update

2019 is here!

After a short coding lull, I'm diving back into the deep end to revisit my final coding project Bee-Z Street with coding partner Patrick Kearney. We've talked many times about the updates we want to make ... the great feedback we've both experienced ... even whether we could one day monetize this labor of love. But I've been working, he's been putting his portfolio in tip-top shape, and our app has remained a WIP.

Until now. Patrick text me last week and said "If we want to finish this app, we need to treat it like a business." I agreed. I received our first meeting agenda today, complete with Roman numerals, deadlines and everything! I'm excited to be coding again, and even more excited to see where we take this amazing project.
