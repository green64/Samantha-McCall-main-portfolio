About Me

As a redheaded, left-handed Scorpio, I was destined to do things differently. I started my career in print publishing, but once I learned HTML, the immediate gratification of making a correction and seeing it online in seconds resonated with me.

I love learning, so I'm very much at home in KU's coding program. With each new skill I acquire, I'm more eager to make the shift from the marketing to the IT side of digital communications. The experience I've already acquired -- content writing, SEO, cross-channel marketing, etc. -- combined with up-to-date experience in full stack programming will undoubtedly dovetail nicely in my future coding roles.

When I'm not researching and writing code, I'm an adoring mom, a cat and all-round animal lover, a die-hard alternative music fan and a semi-successful gardener. I travel whenever I can and am planning my first visit to the great state of Ohio this spring to attend Homecoming in Cincinnati, a festival curated by my favorite band, The National.
