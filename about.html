About Me

Why Fresh Perspective?

Maybe you noticed my blog name or even the "fp18" in my URL ... To me, fresh perspective is the reward for thinking independently.

Take my cover picture. It's awesome, isn't it? Some far-off place full of intrigue and stories ...

Actually, it's in Kansas City! It's a well-known landmark — the WWI Memorial right across from Union Station. But thanks to fresh perspective, it could easily be the portal to another dimension.

As a redheaded left-hander, I was destined to do things differently. I started my career in print publishing, but once I learned HTML, the immediate gratification of making a correction and seeing it online in seconds resonated with me.

When I'm not writing content or researching and writing code, I'm:

  • an adoring mom
  • a cat and all-around animal lover
  • a diehard alternative music fan
  • and a semi-successful gardener

I also travel whenever I can and am thrilled to have a beautiful daughter earning her Ph.D. in Seattle so I have limitless excuses to visit there.

October update

After a short break to see Chloe in Seattle and recover from hernia repair, I'm eager to resume my coding journey. So many dev jobs I see want PHP that I'm going to give the Net Ninja tutorial series a try. I've read that PHP is weird and confusing and inferior to other languages. But it's also the backbone of WordPress. So it seems destined to be around awhile.

It's hard to know what to tackle next when it comes to self-teaching beyond coding school. But it's harder to give up on a goal you've expended time and money toward achieving — and I've spent plenty of both in my pursuit of becoming a full-fledged developer.

After years of building sites freelance and working in marketing roles that included front-end site management, learning another language could be the final rung I need to climb to reach the top of my personal development ladder.
