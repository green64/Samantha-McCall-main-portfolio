About Me

Fresh perspective. It's important to me because it's the payoff for thinking independently. My cover picture is awesome, isn't it? Some far off place full of intrigue and stories ...

Actually, it's in Kansas City! It's a well-known landmark -- the WWI Memorial right across from Union Station. But thanks to fresh perspective, it could easily be the portal to another dimension.

As a redheaded, left-handed Scorpio, I was destined to do things differently. I started my career in print publishing, but once I learned HTML, the immediate gratification of making a correction and seeing it online in seconds resonated with me.

I love learning, so I'm very much at home in KU's coding program. With each new skill I acquire, I'm more eager to make the shift from the marketing to the IT side of digital communications. The experience I've already acquired -- content writing, SEO, cross-channel marketing, etc. -- combined with up-to-date experience in full-stack programming should dovetail nicely in my future coding roles.

When I'm not researching and writing code, I'm an adoring mom, a cat and all-around animal lover, a die-hard alternative music fan and a semi-successful gardener. I travel whenever I can and am planning my first visit to the great state of Ohio this spring to attend Homecoming in Cincinnati, a festival curated by my favorite band, The National.

In the meantime, I'm learning something new EVERY DAY. And this work-in-progress portfolio will reflect that knowledge acquisition.
