An Ode to Parker and Posie

Parker & Posie

were rescue kittens I adopted in 2000. In the slideshow Parker is wearing the polka-dotted collar and Posie has the lion haircut. I'm sure that's not the official groomer name for her trim.

The other cats in the slideshow are rescues too. Lexie's the if-it-fits-I-sits cat, Ninja is the regal tuxedo, Kiko is the sassy Siamese, Pumpkin is the green-eyed robocat, and Snickers and Doodle are "foster fails." They're big boys now and will be really embarrassed to know I used those pics.

Everybody is spayed and neutered, and nobody is allowed to go outside and eat birds. The mice who come inside, however, are fair game.

While this page is a tribute to my cats, it's also another step in my development journey. I wanted to build a page without CDNs; this one uses flex box, CSS grid, and simple HTML. Not bad for a crazy cat lady, amirite?

Parker

Parker was a lady
She always crossed her legs
And as the pretty sister
She never had to beg

She got the softest pillows
She had the freshest treats
And when it came to purring
She had her sister beat

Parker was a sweet girl
With fur between her toes
There never was a kitty
As cute as her in bows

Posie

Posie was a tomboy
She never combed her hair
Her voice was deep and husky
A cat? More like a bear!

Her fur was always messy
She was known to act bazaar
She loved to roll around
When I was playing my guitar

Posie was a sweet girl too
She had a nice long life
Both she and Parker taught me
Black cats are just divine

Parker ~ 2000 - 2013

  

Rest in peace kitty sisters

  

Posie ~ 2000 - 2019
